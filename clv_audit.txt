MLB CLV AUDIT — 2026-06-19
==========================

PART 1 — AUDIT FINDINGS
------------------------

1. OPENING AND CLOSING ODDS CAPTURE
-------------------------------------
No — for MLB, neither exist.

- sync_mlb_odds.py PURGES mlb_odds and mlb_player_prop_odds at the start of every run
  (delete().eq("sport_key", ...)) and then reinserts fresh rows. Only one snapshot ever
  exists in that table — today's, right now. There is no history table, no
  timestamp-keyed append. By the time tomorrow's morning run fires, yesterday's closing
  odds are gone.

- mlb_final_predictions stores the odds_decimal and market_implied_probability at
  pick-generation time (morning run, 8 AM UTC). That IS an implicit opening snapshot —
  but only for the one best pick per game (not all legs of the market).

- mlb_safe_zone stores balanced_novig_pct / banker_novig_pct at pick time. Same story —
  opening is implicitly captured, closing is not.

- There is no "pre-game closing capture" step anywhere in the engine or GitHub Actions.

- mlb_player_prop_odds is also wiped every run. mlb_player_props.best_odds_decimal is
  the only prop-price snapshot.


2. CLV COMPUTATION PER GRADED PICK
------------------------------------
No. Completely absent for MLB. The existing CLV files:

- build_clv_tracking.py — FAKE PLACEHOLDER: hardcodes opening_odds = -110,
  closing_odds = -115. Writes to soccer_clv_tracking. Not called from anywhere in the
  MLB pipeline.

- build_real_clv_tracking.py — queries soccer_odds (not MLB), groups by
  (game_id, market, selection, bookmaker), takes first/last row by captured_at.
  Formula: clv_diff = closing_odds_decimal - opening_odds_decimal — this is comparing
  raw decimals, not de-vigged probs. That is wrong (confuses odds format with
  probability). For soccer only.

- build_soccer_clv_tracker.py — soccer only, uses soccer_live_odds_snapshots.
  Formula: ((opening - closing) / opening) * 100 — also not a proper CLV formula.

- mlb_pick_grades schema: no CLV columns.
- mlb_pick_results schema: no CLV columns.

4. WHAT'S MISSING / BROKEN
----------------------------
- No append-only snapshot table for MLB odds. The wipe-and-replace pattern destroys
  history on every run.

- No closing-odds capture step. The hourly refresh-batter-props job does not call
  sync_mlb_odds.

- No CLV computation module for MLB.

- No CLV storage columns in mlb_pick_grades or mlb_pick_results.

- The correct CLV formula (de-vig both prices,
  CLV = closing_novig_prob − opening_novig_prob) is not implemented anywhere.

- Props CLV: structurally not feasible without both legs of the prop market at
  closing — excluded by design.

- build_clv_tracking.py uses hardcoded fake American odds (-110/-115).
  This file should not be run.

- build_real_clv_tracking.py uses raw decimal differences (not de-vigged probs)
  and operates on soccer data only. The formula is incorrect.

CLV FORMULA (implemented in build_mlb_clv.py)
----------------------------------------------
  For a 2-way market (legs A and B at decimal odds dec_A, dec_B):

    raw_A = 1 / dec_A
    raw_B = 1 / dec_B
    total = raw_A + raw_B
    novig_A = (raw_A / total) * 100   <- de-vigged implied probability (%)

  CLV = closing_novig_prob - opening_novig_prob   (in percentage points)

  Positive CLV: market moved toward our pick after publication.
  Negative CLV: market moved against our pick.

  Picks excluded from CLV stats (not counted as 0):
    - no-odds picks (edge_flag = 'no-odds')
    - VOID grades
    - picks with no closing snapshot (alternate-line safe-zone, early-season games)
    - player props (no 2-sided closing market available)
